Pipeline

Four stages, one ranked list

From a job description to a shortlist, without a spreadsheet in between.

01 · UPLOAD

Bring the role in

Upload a job description as a file, select one already saved, or describe it to the AI assistant and let it draft the posting.

02 · PARSE

Read every resume

Pattern extraction pull name, contact details, skills, and years of experience out of each PDF or DOCX.

03 · MATCH

Score against the role

Sentence-Transformer embeddings compare meaning, not just words — weighted 40% skills, 40% experience, 20% semantic fit.

04 · RANK

One ordered shortlist

Every candidate lands in a single ranked view, highest match first, ready to export or move to interview.
